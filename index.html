Research

MSc THESIS
Human Pose Estimation and Action Classification Based on Deep Learning Algorithm
UNIVERSITI MALAYSIA PERLIS (UNIMAP) · COMPUTER ENGINEERING · 2024–PRESENT
Develops a real-time pipeline for monitoring worker safety at industrial sites using CCTV input. Two YOLO models run in parallel: YOLOv11x detects PPE compliance (hard hats, safety shoes, gloves, welding masks), while YOLOv8x-pose extracts 17 skeletal keypoints per person to classify posture and verify equipment is worn on the correct body region — not just present in frame.

Training data collected during Petronas site visits in Kluang and Kasawari, Johor. Augmentation strategies tuned with Optuna Bayesian optimisation, improving mAP@0.5 from 51.8% (baseline) to 75.4% across five PPE classes.
YOLOv11x YOLOv8x-pose Optuna PPE Detection Action Classification Petronas Dataset
COLLABORATION
Sleeping Posture Classification for Sleep Apnea Detection
HOSPITAL RESEARCH COLLABORATION · UNIMAP · ONGOING
Applies computer vision and ML to classify sleeping positions from overhead IR footage — contributing the posture classification component to a hospital-led sleep apnea study.

A custom rule-based pose classifier uses YOLOv8-pose keypoints to distinguish Supine, Prone, Lateral Left, and Lateral Right positions. Includes a per-session self-calibration stage for body-size normalisation. Three ML models benchmarked: XGBoost, LSTM, and XGBoost+LSTM hybrid. Results exported per-subject to Excel for clinical review.
YOLOv8-pose XGBoost · LSTM · Hybrid IR Camera Sleep Apnea Clinical Research
